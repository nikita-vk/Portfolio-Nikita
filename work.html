Work Experience

Biocon Biologics

Quality Control Microbiology Laboratory Intern

For the first week at this internship, I was trained on standard operating procedures (SOPs) of the laboratory, collecting air and surface samples around production sites, performing Bacterial spread plating, conducting MALDI/TOF based mass spectroscopy to identify the microbes, and conducting Bacterial Endotoxin Tests (BET) on the pharmaceuticals produced by Biocon Biologics (Insugen and Trastuzumab). After training, I shadowed the principal scientist for a few days before I was able to perform these tasks by myself. By the end of my internship, I collected 30+ samples to perform bacterial identification through bacterial spread plating, gram staining, and mass spectroscopy. I was further trained in protein extraction from fungi by observing the procedure for 100+ samples, membrane filtering techniques to test Water for Injection testing (WFI), sterility testing for injectable drugs, and injectables designing and other drug delivery device design at the R&D department. This internship was very fast paced and this made the experience very exciting because I was able to gain an incredible amount of skills and knowledge in such a short duration of time.


Skills I have gained and developed from this Experience:

  • Bacterial Endotoxin Testing
  • Gram-Staining
  • MALDI/TOF Mass-spectroscopy
  • Sterility Testing
  • Quality Control
  • Bacterial Spread plating
  • BioQuell Cube
  • Autoclave
  • Plate Readers
  • Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP)

First-Year Student Outreach Project (FYSOP)

Staff Leader

FYSOP, created by the Boston University Community Service Center, aims to inspire students to become active community members through reflection and acts of service. Specifically designed for incoming freshmen, I took on this role as a rising sophomore. My responsibilities included fostering connections and friendships among the students on my team, boosting team morale, organizing visits to community service centers, actively participating in service activities, facilitating post-service reflections, and serving as a mentor throughout the year.
